What we believe
01

Writing over
metrics.

No follower counts in your face. No algorithmic feeds pushing what's trending. The only number that matters is zero — as in zero distractions between you and the page.

02

Readers over
impressions.

We don't sell eyeballs to advertisers. We build a place where readers actually read — because they chose to be here, not because they were algorithmically nudged.

03

Craft over
velocity.

There's no publish button pressure here. Write slowly. Edit ruthlessly. Publish when it's ready. The platform will wait — forever, if that's what it takes.

"Good writing doesn’t need an algorithm to find its audience.  It needs a home."

The person behind it

Michael
Pedemonte

mchlpdmnt ↗

Developer and writer. Built Blogton in 2026 because every other platform was optimising for the wrong thing.

The core idea is embarrassingly simple: a place where writing is the product, not the bait. No ads, no dark patterns, no growth hacking. Just a clean editor, a reader-first feed, and the belief that good sentences still matter.

Blogton is in active development. Every feature is weighed against one question — does this make the writing better? If the answer is no, it doesn't ship.
